Corrugated Belts and Drum Lagging

Corrugated Belts: We offer a variety of corrugator belts that have been specially developed for use in double facers in corrugating systems. The belts are exposed to substantial stresses from temperature, contact pressure, friction, tensile stress and moisture. The belts consist of multiple layer fabrics.
